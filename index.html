Who am I?

Hi, I'm Brandon Baek, a 15-year-old Korean-American freshman in California. My second-generation immigrant background and Dual Language Immersion (DLI) experience have shaped my resilience and cultural awareness, making me proficient in both Korean and English.

As an ambivert, I balance technical and creative passions. I develop AI/ML models and create humorous content on TikTok and YouTube. I also enjoy baking, running, and hiking, embracing challenges for personal growth.

I'm deeply committed to community impact. I serve as Vice President of the La Crescenta branch of the Korean American Music Academy (KAMA), a nonprofit organization founded in 1989 with approximately 100 members across four branches. KAMA donates to various causes, reinforcing the importance of giving back. I was also 8th-grade class president at Rosemont (1600 students), and I'm a member of Homemade Delights, supporting mental health awareness. My goal is to use AI/ML for positive global change, potentially through a nonprofit organization, inspired by the community-focused values I've learned.

Story

Programming has profoundly impacted my life. Even as a toddler, while other kids drew artistic masterpieces, I was heavily captivated by drawing simple rectangles and lines connecting everything, attempting to create “systems” with my imagination. This fascination led me to self-teach coding, beginning with Scratch at the age of nine.

Coding quickly evolved into a passionate hobby, filling my free time, but I suddenly felt a slight discomfort with its perceived limitations. While I loved problem-solving, the primary career path seemed confined to building websites and apps—a direction that didn't fully resonate. And since my primary language, Python, I couldn’t even do that. This somewhat discouraged me for a time.

However, my interest was reignited during a math class when the teacher introduced Linear Regression, the idea of predicting the future with math. I was immediately captivated. I implemented it and found it incredibly exciting. This sparked my deep dive into Data Science, AI, and ML, during which I unknowingly developed a word predictor, a rudimentary form of what we now call a language model, the foundation to ChatGPT. The introduction of ChatGPT later illuminated the true potential of these models.

I aspire to become an AI/ML Engineer. My fascination with "predicting the future," and with chatbots and AI in particular, has been a constant since childhood. From those early attempts to create chatbots in Scratch, which later evolved into more sophisticated Python projects involving advanced text similarity, word-type tokenization, and text topic detection, I've consistently worked towards this goal for the past five years. In January of 2025, I successfully developed and trained an LLM (Basically ChatGPT) from scratch. My dream is to democratize AI's support capabilities, ensuring that anyone can benefit from its advanced assistance in fields such as medicine, law, finance, education, and agriculture.
